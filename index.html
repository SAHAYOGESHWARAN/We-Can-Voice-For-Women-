We Can Voice for Women Foundation

About the Organization

The We Can Voice for Women Foundation is a grassroots non-governmental organization (NGO) committed to fostering gender equity and empowering women across India. Established in 2019, the Foundation was born out of a deep-seated belief that women’s empowerment is a national priority essential for inclusive and sustainable development.

We believe true empowerment is multi-dimensional and must address education, healthcare, economic independence, legal protection, and social inclusion. Our mission is to dismantle societal barriers and build ecosystems where women can thrive with dignity and autonomy.

Our programs uplift women from marginalized communities by equipping them with knowledge, skills, resources, and confidence to lead independent lives. We actively promote male allyship as vital partners in the journey toward gender justice.

Vision

To create a society where women are enlightened, healthy, and empowered, capable of making informed choices, leading independent lives, and contributing meaningfully to nation-building.

Vision Pillars

  • Women Enlightenment: Equal access to quality education and literacy.
  • Women Enhealthment: Promoting physical and mental health awareness and preventive care.
  • Women Empowerment: Enabling economic independence through skills development and entrepreneurship.

Mission

Our mission is to provide holistic support systems that empower women to live with independence, dignity, and purpose. We create safe, inclusive, resource-rich environments enabling women to overcome socio-economic barriers and actively contribute to their communities.

Core Focus Areas

  • Education & Literacy Initiatives: Promoting lifelong learning and school/college enrollment.
  • Health & Well-being Programs: Addressing menstrual hygiene, maternal health, and mental wellness.
  • Livelihood & Economic Empowerment: Vocational training, entrepreneurship support, job placement.
  • Gender Equality & Advocacy: Campaigns, awareness drives, and legal aid for women's rights.
  • Community Engagement: Mobilizing local communities to challenge gender norms and support inclusion.
  • Counseling & Support Services: Emotional and psychological support for women in crisis.

Target Beneficiaries

  • Women and girls from marginalized and underserved communities
  • Survivors of domestic violence and gender-based discrimination
  • Unemployed or underemployed women seeking economic opportunities
  • Adolescent girls needing health and reproductive education
  • Rural women needing literacy and skill development

Geographic Focus

Primary operations in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, with expanding outreach to neighboring districts and rural regions of South India.

Partnerships & Collaborations

  • Government Agencies and Organizations
  • Local and International NGOs
  • CSR Partners
  • Academic Institutions
  • Health and Legal Professionals
  • Media and Advocacy Groups
